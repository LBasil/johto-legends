Welcome to the un-official documentation website for Pokemon Johto Legends

The story

Journey across the Johto region 15 years before Gold/Kris's Pokémon adventure. You'll meet new faces, as well as familiar ones. Choose from Dratini or Larvitar as your starters! Aspire to collect the 8 badges, challenge the Pokémon League, and record all known Pokémon in your Journal! Beware, for there is a conspiracy at large! Strange people in black have been poaching and kidnapping Pokémon across the region! It may be up to you to solve this mystery! This current version lets you obtain 7 badges.

Some features

  • 311 Pokémon from Generations 1-4, as well as Sylveon, Cursola, and the Fakémon Skarmini, prevolution to Skarmory.
  • 395 moves from across all Generations, including an original move, Steel Slice.
  • Physical/Special Split.
  • Fairy Type has been added.
  • Steel no longer resists Dark and Ghost, and is now up to date.
  • Ice now resists Water and Electric in addition to Ice.
  • Stats pages now include the Pokémon's DVs and EVs, as well as a fourth page for the Pokémon's caught time, location, level, and friendship.
  • New evolution methods, such as a Link Cable item for the Trade Evolutions, and other evolution items such as Metal Coat now act like evolution stones.
  • 70 TMs and 17 Move Tutor moves. TMs now have infinite uses.
  • HMs have been replaced by Key Items.
