Version 0.2.6

  • Signal Wave is learned by Butterfree, Venomoth and Yanmega
  • Poke Balls with TMs are now blue
  • Gym Leaders have been buffed
  • A haircut guy has been added to Olivine Cafe
  • Tutors now require payment, ranging from 800 to 2500
  • Electirizer and Magmarizer are available at the Black Market
